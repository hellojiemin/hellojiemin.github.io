Weaving magic with her words:

Writings


Magazine

A class of 19 students embarked on a 35-day trip to Tianjin, China for their Feature Writing module. As the editor of her magazine team, Jie Min worked closely with her four other team mates to create an industry-quality magazine.


The articles Drum Tower Street, Paper Cutting and Their Cup of Tea are written either fully or in-part by Jie Min. The other articles have all been refined by her and the sub-editors of her team.


Take a look at the Editor's Note, written by Jie Min, to begin your experience of Tianjin through the pages.
