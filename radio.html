The theatre of the mind:

Radio


Aside from being a host at Radio Heatwave, Ngee Ann Polytechnic's campus radio station, Jie Min takes on another role in the theatre of the mind. She scripts, voices and produces audio content, because getting good at such a
large-scale medium is very important for storytelling.


Radio Commercial for Crescendo the musical

One of Jie Min's most notable works is the LOVE 97.2FM radio commercial for Shining Entertainment's musical Crescendo. Jie Min scripted and voiced for the commercial. Under the guidance of UFM 100.3 DJ and lecturer Teo Ying and in collaboration with her senior Shane, the client accepted the pitch and was very satisfied with the final product.


The commercial went on to be broadcasted on 97.2FM, a national radio station and received good feedback from the client.
